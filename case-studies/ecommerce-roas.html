The Challenge

A fast-growing fashion e-commerce brand was spending $15,000/month on Facebook and Instagram ads but barely breaking even. Their ROAS was stuck at 1.8x, and their cost per acquisition was climbing every month.

The main issues were:

  • Broad, untargeted audiences wasting ad spend
  • No retargeting strategy for cart abandoners
  • Manual campaign management eating up time
  • Poor creative testing process

Our Solution

We implemented a complete AI-powered advertising system:

1. AI Audience Targeting

Used machine learning to analyze their best customers and create lookalike audiences that actually converted. We segmented by purchase behavior, not just demographics.

2. Automated Retargeting Flows

Built dynamic retargeting campaigns that showed personalized product ads to cart abandoners within 1 hour. Added WhatsApp automation for high-value carts.

3. Creative Optimization AI

Automated A/B testing of ad creatives and automatically paused underperformers. The AI learned which styles, colors, and messaging worked best.
