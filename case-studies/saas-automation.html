The Challenge

A B2B SaaS platform was generating plenty of demo requests, but their small sales team was overwhelmed. Most leads were unqualified, and the manual follow-up process meant deals took 60+ days to close.

  • Sales team spending 70% of time on unqualified leads
  • No automated lead nurturing between touchpoints
  • Long gaps in communication causing drop-offs
  • Manual data entry slowing everything down

Our Solution

1. AI Chatbot for Instant Qualification

Deployed an AI chatbot that asked qualifying questions 24/7. It scored leads based on company size, budget, and urgency—routing hot leads directly to sales.

2. Automated Email Nurturing

Created personalized drip campaigns triggered by user behavior. If someone watched a demo video but didn't book a call, they got a case study email the next day.

3. CRM Integration & Auto-Logging

Connected everything to their CRM. Every chatbot conversation, email open, and demo attendance was automatically logged—no manual data entry.
