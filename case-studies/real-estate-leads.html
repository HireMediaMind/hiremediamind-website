The Challenge

A Dubai-based real estate agency was spending AED 50,000/month on Facebook and Google ads. The problem? 80% of leads were tire-kickers—people browsing with no real intent to buy.

  • Agents wasting time on unqualified leads
  • No way to identify serious buyers upfront
  • Slow response times (hours, not minutes)
  • High ad spend with low conversion rates

Our Solution

1. AI Lead Scoring System

Built an AI model that scored every lead based on budget, timeline, location preference, and engagement. Only leads scoring 7+ went to agents.

2. WhatsApp Automation

Instant WhatsApp messages sent to new leads with property details, virtual tour links, and a chatbot that answered common questions 24/7.

3. Smart Follow-Up Sequences

Automated follow-ups based on lead behavior. If someone viewed a property page 3+ times, they got a personalized video message from an agent.
