What is Performance Marketing?

Performance Marketing is running paid advertising campaigns (Facebook Ads, Google Ads) where you only pay for results - clicks, leads, or sales. Unlike traditional advertising, you can track exactly how much revenue each dollar generates.

We manage your entire ad strategy: campaign setup, daily optimization, A/B testing, audience targeting, and conversion tracking. Our goal is to maximize your ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) - meaning every dollar you spend on ads generates maximum revenue.
