How The Stack Fits Together

Five layers work in sync so there are zero leaks between marketing, sales, and success.

  1. Layer 1

    Acquisition Engine

    Full-funnel campaigns across Meta, Google, YouTube, LinkedIn, Programmatic. Creative personalization by persona + region.

  2. Layer 2

    Lead Capture + Enrichment

    Landing pages, chatbots, WhatsApp, and native lead forms with instant enrichment (Clearbit, Apollo, ZoomInfo) and validation.

  3. Layer 3

    AI Qualification & Routing

    Conversation intelligence, scoring models, and routing rules push hot leads to CRM/Slack instantly with context.

  4. Layer 4

    Lifecycle Automation

    Nurture sequences, appointment booking, deal desk approvals, onboarding, and success workflows built in n8n/HubSpot/Marketo.

  5. Layer 5

    Revenue Intelligence

    Unified dashboards with ROAS, CAC, payback, LTV, cohort analysis, and forecasting tied to finance targets.
